Toby Fox

Toby Fox is an American game developer and composer, most well known for his work on Undertale and Deltarune, and for being a composer on Homestuck and Hiveswap. He was previously known as Radiation, and is the founder of the Homestuck Music Contribution club and he is a contributor to "A Profound Waste of Time" magazine.

Toby Fox is important for creating indie games like Undertale and Deltarune. Both are known for their storytelling, creative gameplay, and emotional depth. He is also a composer, with his music having strong emotions and has a blend of creativity and nostalgia. Toby Fox has had an impact on the gaming industry through his unique and independent development.
