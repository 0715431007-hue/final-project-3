Toby Fox

Robert F. "Toby" Fox (born in Manchester, NH on October 11, 1991) is an American game developer and composer, most well known for his work on Undertale and Deltarune, and for being a composer on Homestuck and Hiveswap. He was previously known as Radiation. He is the founder of the Homestuck Music Contribution club aswell as a contributor to "A Profound Waste of Time" magazine.

Toby Fox is important for creating indie games like Undertale and Deltarune. Both are known for their storytelling, creative gameplay, and emotional depth. He is also a composer, with his music having strong emotions and has a blend of creativity and nostalgia. Toby Fox has had an impact on the gaming industry through his unique and independent development.

The Annoying Dog is a silly white dog from the video games Undertale and Deltarune. This dog serves as an in game character for Toby Fox, this dog appears to make chaos and disrupt the games' events, it also appears to troll characters and steal items and be a humorous version of Toby Fox himself. Toby Fox chose to have a dog as his in game avatar because Temmie Chang loved dogs.
